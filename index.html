Meet Your Sales Coach

Hi, I'm Dave Sherman, and I've been selling for as long as I can remember, literally. My first "sales job" was at seven years old, going door to door, selling raffle tickets for one of my mom's charities. By 1980, I was knocking on doors again, this time selling $600 vacuum cleaners. It was tough, but it taught me lessons about persistence, confidence, and connecting with people that I still use today.

For the past 20+ years, I've shifted my focus to helping others succeed in sales. I've trained and coached thousands of beginners, people just like you, who felt nervous, unprepared, or unsure if they had what it takes to sell. Watching them transform from hesitant to confident is the reason I love what I do.

I created A Beginner's Guide to Successful Selling because I know how overwhelming sales can seem when you're starting out. My goal is simple: to make sales easy to understand, approachable, and practical, so you can take your first steps toward success with confidence.
